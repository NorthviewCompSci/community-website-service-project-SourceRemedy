Years

Freshman

This year focuses on getting involved, and getting to know yourself and what you believe in. This is done through:

  • The power of reflection and setting goals.
  • Getting involved in something.
  • Identifying personal values.
  • Attending leadership lunches featuring community leaders.

Sophomore

This year focuses on the value of service. This is achieved through:

  • Joining Interact.
  • Class service projects.
  • Attending leadership lunches featuring community leaders.

Junior

This year focuses on the Leadership Experience. This is executed through:

  • Defining leadership.
  • Understanding the value of teamwork.
  • Creating a vision (developing, communication, action planning, sharing, building relationships, and leading with integrity).
  • Learning the components of effective communication.
  • Leadership skill/Personality assesments.
  • Attending leadership lunches featuring community leaders.

Senior

This year focuses on leadership in action. This is accomplished through:

  • Establishing leadership roles within the Northview family or Sylvania community.
  • Reflecting on the leadership experience.
  • Participating in an etiquette dinner and toastmaster.
  • Engaging in a public speaking competition.
  • Attending leadership lunches featuring community leaders.
