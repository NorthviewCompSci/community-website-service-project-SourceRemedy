Description

The Leadership Academy has a variety of experiences, throughout its four year course, that it would like to help all of its students take a part in. These experiences include, but are not limited to:

  • Learning about who you are and what you believe in.
  • Learning about how to work as part of a team.
  • Meeting with community leaders and learning from their experiences.
  • Building the skills necessary to be an effective leader.
  • Getting a chance to develop a leadership philosophy, put it into practice, and reflect on it.
  • Recieve a recognition at graduation!
